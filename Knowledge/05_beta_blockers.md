# Beta Blockers

## Carvedilol
- Starting dose: 3.125 mg PO BID
- Incremental doses: 3.125 mg → 6.25 mg → 12.5 mg → 25 mg PO BID
- Maximum dose: 25 mg BID (50 mg BID if >85 kg)

## Metoprolol Succinate (ER)
- Starting dose: 12.5–25 mg PO daily
- Incremental doses: 12.5 → 25 → 50 → 100 → 200 mg PO daily
- Maximum dose: 200 mg PO daily

## Bisoprolol
- Starting dose: 1.25 mg PO daily
- Incremental doses: 1.25 → 2.5 → 5 → 10 mg PO daily
- Maximum dose: 10 mg PO daily

### Contraindications
- HR <50 bpm
- AV block (2nd/3rd degree)
- Sick sinus syndrome
- Severe HF needing inotropes
- Severe asthma/bronchospasm
- Cardiogenic shock

### Hold / Discontinue If
- HR <50 (hold/reduce)
- HR <45 (hold/discontinue)
- Symptomatic bradycardia
- New AV block
- SBP <80–85 mmHg symptomatic
- Acute decompensated HF
- Severe bronchospasm
