# Hydralazine / Isosorbide Dinitrate

## Hydralazine
- Starting dose: 25 mg PO TID
- Incremental doses: 25 → 37.5 → 50 → 75 mg PO TID
- Maximum dose: 75 mg PO TID (up to 100 mg TID)

## Isosorbide Dinitrate
- Starting dose: 20 mg PO TID
- Incremental doses: 20 → 30 → 40 mg PO TID
- Maximum dose: 40 mg PO TID

## Fixed-Dose Combination (BiDil)
- Each tablet: Hydralazine 37.5 mg + Isosorbide Dinitrate 20 mg
- Starting dose: 1 tab PO TID
- Incremental doses: 1 → 2 tabs PO TID
- Maximum dose: 2 tabs PO TID

### Contraindications
- SBP <85–90 mmHg symptomatic
- PDE-5 inhibitor use
- Recent MI (<48 hr)
- Drug-induced lupus

### Hold / Discontinue If
- Hypotension or orthostasis
- Drug-induced lupus
- Severe headache
- Tachycardia >110–120 bpm
