# SGLT-2 Inhibitors

## Dapagliflozin
- Starting dose: 10 mg PO daily
- Maximum dose: 10 mg PO daily

## Empagliflozin
- Starting dose: 10 mg PO daily
- Maximum dose: 10 mg PO daily

## Sotagliflozin
- Starting dose: 200 mg PO daily (if eGFR ≥25)
- Incremental doses: 200 → 400 mg PO daily
- Maximum dose: 400 mg PO daily

### Contraindications
- eGFR <20 (dapagliflozin, empagliflozin)
- eGFR <25 (sotagliflozin)
- Type 1 diabetes
- DKA history
- Dialysis

### Hold / Discontinue If
- eGFR below threshold
- DKA or euglycemic DKA
- Severe dehydration
- Recurrent UTI or genital infection
- Fournier’s gangrene
- AKI (temporary hold)
