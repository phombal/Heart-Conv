# Soluble Guanylate Cyclase (sGC) Stimulator

## Vericiguat
- Starting dose: 2.5 mg PO daily
- Incremental doses: 2.5 → 5 → 10 mg PO daily (q2wk)
- Maximum dose: 10 mg PO daily

### Contraindications
- PDE-5 inhibitor use
- Pregnancy
- Severe hepatic impairment (Child-Pugh C)

### Hold / Discontinue If
- SBP <90 mmHg symptomatic
- Symptomatic hypotension
- Concurrent PDE-5 inhibitor need
- Pregnancy
- Worsening anemia
